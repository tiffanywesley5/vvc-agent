ABOUT VELVET VISION CREATIVE

Velvet Vision Creative is a creative studio founded by Tiffany Snow — a full-stack developer, licensed esthetician, and cosmetic formulator who's built beauty brands from formulation to shelf.

Before writing a single line of code, Tiffany spent seven years at Block (formerly Square), where her instinct for technology sharpened into a skillset. She's a graduate of Savvy Coders, a serial entrepreneur, and someone who's been building businesses from the ground up for as long as she can remember. She doesn't just understand the tools  she understands the industries they're built for.

VVC sits at the intersection of design, technology, and strategic thinking. We believe AI tools should be accessible to every founder, not just the ones with engineering teams. And because our founder has been the founder on the other side of the table  formulating products, licensing, launching  we build with a perspective most studios can't offer.
